This page contains general information about invite letter to obtain visa to attend the conference in India. Please note delegates are advised to consult with the Indian embassy and consulate services in their home country regarding visa requirements:

Details required:
  • Full Name as in passport
  • Passport Number
  • Passport Validity – From & To Date

Details to be provided to the Organizing chairs – Samuel Dorairaj samuel.v.dorairaj@intel.com & Lata Prabhu lprabhu@qti.qualcomm.com
